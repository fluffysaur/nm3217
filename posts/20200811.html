Shower Thoughts

Streams of blue sprout thoughts so free; just like watering a tree

Posted by Tan Yi Jia on August 11, 2020

Have you discovered the magic of the shower?

There's something about those streams of water that makes it a place where creativity thrives.

Isn't it ironic? For creativity to thrive in a space so confined?

I find most ideas come to me in the shower. Bug fixes for an application, ideas for a short film - anything. Pausing in this confined box as water flows down my cheeks lets me think of thoughts outside (which is why I drew the lightbulb beyond the shower space).

A cup of coffee in a café and a laptop with with a million tabs of design ideas - one can argue that this is sufficient for creativity, for inspiration to flow within.

But it's in the shower where these thoughts are processed, refined, and turned into something uniquely mine.

So whenever I feel lost; whenever a billion browser tabs, or a hundred cups of coffee, or a long stroll in the park don't seem to stimulate my bi-polar brain,

I treat it to some magic in the shower.

To go places and do things that have never been done before – that’s what living is all about.
